President's Message


It is a great pleasure to welcome you to the , official website of People's Initiative of Enhancement(PIE). PIE, have been started with the motto to eradicate child labour & human trafficking from the society. We have restlessly worked on environmental and all social issues,when the society needed it immediately. We have provided ourselves to stand always by the society reluctantly for all matter needed for it's betterment and upliftment.The challeges before PIE are many we are on day-to-day mission towards meeting the same through a social blend of human resources and hardwork.With unimaginable hardwork and efforts in the past few years we have grown slowly building up a strong and hardworking organisation and yes, we will continue to be so.

    I request all citizens to come forward and join us in this mission.
                                                                    With Regards

WELCOME TO PIE

People's Initiative For Enhancement - (PIE) is a city based NGO started on 21.01.2016 with the motto to "WORK TOGETHER" and to have a child labour free society, a GREEN society and a society which would portray bright ourself on the issue of Women Empowerment, Youth Empowerment and Communal Harmony.PIE is the brain child of the present President Mr.Safiur Rahaman and Hon.Secretary Mr Avinash Almal who thought have an organization which would not only do social service ,but STAND by the Society and for the society whenever she needs them.Now the Organisation is a proud family of more than 50 members having an effective generating body.PIE, being new to this sector is now mainly focusing on Youth issues and plans to holds seminar and workshops on them.
Do come and get associated with us.Its not only MINE but YOURS NGO too. A perfect TEAMWORK in the regards will surely lead to the betterment of society.
